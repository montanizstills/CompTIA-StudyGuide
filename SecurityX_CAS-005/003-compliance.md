# Awareness of Industry Specific Compliance

## Healthcare
- HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)
  - Privacy Rule and Security Rule for protected health information (PHI)
  - Covers healthcare providers, health plans, and business associates
  - Requirements for safeguarding electronic PHI (ePHI)
  - Breach notification requirements
- HITECH (Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act)
  - Enhanced HIPAA penalties and enforcement
  - Promotes adoption of electronic health records
- Medical device security requirements (FDA guidelines)

## Financial
- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley)
  - Financial reporting controls
  - IT controls supporting financial systems
- GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act)
  - Safeguards Rule for protecting customer financial information
- NYDFS Cybersecurity Regulation (23 NYCRR 500)
  - Requirements for financial institutions operating in New York
  - Risk assessments, documentation of policies, designation of CISO
- PCI DSS (specific to payment card data)
- FINRA (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority) requirements

## Government
- FISMA (Federal Information Security Modernization Act)
  - Risk-based security for federal agencies
- FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program)
  - Cloud security assessment and authorization
- CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification)
  - Defense contractors requirements
  - Five maturity levels of cybersecurity practices
- State and local government requirements
- Special considerations for classified information

## Utilities/Critical Infrastructure
- NERC CIP (North American Electric Reliability Corporation Critical Infrastructure Protection)
  - Standards for electric utility industry
  - Protection of critical cyber assets
- Federal Energy Regulatory Commission
- Water/wastewater system security
- TSA Pipeline Security Guidelines
- NRC (Nuclear Regulatory Commission) requirements
- SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) security requirements

# Industry Standards

## PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
- 12 major requirements organized in 6 control objectives
- Scope determination and segmentation concepts
- Self-assessment questionnaires (SAQs) and reporting
- Service provider responsibilities
- Compensating controls documentation
- Annual validation requirements

## ISO/IEC 27000 Series
- ISO/IEC 27001: Information security management systems (ISMS)
  - Requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an ISMS
- ISO/IEC 27002: Code of practice for information security controls
- ISO/IEC 27017: Cloud security
- ISO/IEC 27018: Protection of PII in public clouds
- ISO/IEC 27701: Privacy information management
- ISO/IEC 31000: Risk management framework

## Digital Markets Act (DMA)
- EU regulation targeting "gatekeepers" in digital markets
- Interoperability requirements
- Data usage limitations
- Fair business practices requirements
- Compliance obligations for large online platforms

## Additional Standards to Know

## Security Organization Control Type 2 (SOC2)
- Five trust service criteria (TSC):
  - Security (common criteria)
  - Availability
  - Processing integrity
  - Confidentiality
  - Privacy

  - Type 1: Point-in-time assessment
  - Type 2: Assessment over a period (typically 6-12 months)

- Audit preparation and management
- Service organization vs. user entity responsibilities
